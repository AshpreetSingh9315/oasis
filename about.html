About Us

Welcome to OASIS IMPEX

OASIS IMPEX, Established in the year 2023 is a registered company under MSME, AP Chamber of commerce, primarily as a Merchant Export Company in Pune Maharashtra (India). Its one of the leading and reputed exporters dealing with wide range of commodities and services ranging from Areca Leaf Plates, Cow Dung Cakes, Cocopeat Bricks, Natural Mehandi Cones, Coconuts, Onion, Pomegranates, Turmeric Fingure, Dry Fruits, Banana, Onion Powder, Garlic Powder, Coconut Brooms, Imitation Jewellary, Basmati Rice and Maize etc. catering to the wide range of customers across the globe.

We have been sourcing and procuring high quality products from a number of producers across the country and deliver them to global clients. Our cordial and long term relations with our customers & our efforts in delivering quality products & customized services have enabled us to attract the clients from the national and international market.
